About

Magna Cum Laude, BSCS — currently leading IT initiatives for NISU's Supreme Student Council.

I'm a Computer Science student at Northern Iloilo State University, graduating Magna Cum Laude, who also handles most of the creative and publication work for my university's student government — from LED wall visuals for campus-wide events to promo videos for the university itself.

Outside the editing suite, I've built an automatic parking system with sensors, a face-recognition attendance system, and a thesis app that detects the freshness of pen shells (tinga) using machine learning. I also play keyboard for my university's band, placing top finishes at RASUC regional band battles.

I'm looking for IT-related opportunities — technical support, video editing, social media, or computer maintenance — where I can put both the technical and creative sides of what I do to work.
